## Why Each Implementation Choice Was Made

- Manual CSV/JSON provider first: works without credentials and avoids provider-term risk.
- Search provider stubs: preserves architecture for future official APIs without brittle scraping.
- Metadata-first design: keeps provenance, rights review, quality state, and human review traceable.
- Perceptual hash plus exact hash: catches byte-identical duplicates and visually similar candidates cheaply.
- Soft review buckets: prevents over-rejecting useful but imperfect candidates.
- Anomaly triage as pending-safe hook: respects that anomaly artifacts are not fully available yet and avoids fake labels.
- Local weights path: directly addresses the restricted environment blocker from Prompt 2.
